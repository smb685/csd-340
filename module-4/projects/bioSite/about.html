About Latoya

Latoya is a driven and accomplished professional who currently works as a QA Engineer. She has always been motivated by curiosity and a dedication to growth, values that have shaped both her career and personal journey. After earning her Master's Degree in Health Administration, Latoya applied her knowledge and determination toward making an impact in both technology and healthcare spaces.

Her commitment to excellence has extended beyond the classroom. Latoya contributed her voice and perspective as a Co-Author in the book Can't Tell Me Nothing, a project that allowed her to share her insights with a wider audience. She was also honored by the Red Cross Mid-Alabama Chapter with the Biomedical Achievement Award, recognizing her contributions and leadership in advancing healthcare and community support.

Outside of her professional accomplishments, Latoya finds joy and balance through her hobbies. She loves immersing herself in books, constantly expanding her knowledge and imagination through reading. Writing is another passion of hers, offering a creative outlet to share ideas, stories, and reflections. Together, these hobbies reflect her thoughtful nature and her ability to connect deeply with both the technical and human sides of life.

As she continues her career and personal journey, Latoya remains focused on growth, excellence, and impact. She inspires those around her with her persistence, achievements, and dedication to living a meaningful life.
